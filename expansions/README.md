# Expansions — Optional Packs for a Growing Foundry

The Solo Path is the spine: stories → patterns → principles →
practice → foundations. Nothing in this folder is required to walk
it.

These packs exist for a different moment — when your Foundry is far
enough along that you want to *use* it for something specific. Each
one states its own prerequisite honestly. If you don't meet the gate
yet, that's not falling behind; it's the same answer the whole path
runs on: not yet, keep observing.

| Pack | What you build | Ready when you have |
|---|---|---|
| [**The Foundry Council**](council/) | A council of AI voices that argue from your own evidence, plus a Weaver that forces one decision | 2–3 story cards, a pattern hunch, an Open Questions page (Stage 1+) |
| [**Practice Documents**](practice-documents.md) | Versioned files that capture how you produce a recurring deliverable — so quality stops depending on re-explaining yourself | A deliverable that's recurred, and at least one named failure (Stage 4-ish) |
| [**Teach the AI Your Voice**](teach-the-ai-your-voice.md) | Standing instructions built from your Language Page, so AI drafts start from your voice instead of the house voice | A Language Page with genuinely recurring phrases (Stage 4) |

The three share one principle, and it's worth saying once here:
**rules in files, not requests in prompts.** A Foundry that lives in
your head has to be re-explained forever. A Foundry that lives in
files compounds.

*A note on the Council pack: it also runs as a live buildathon
session. If you're planning to attend one, skip the pack until after —
the live build is better the first time.*
