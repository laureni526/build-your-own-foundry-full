# The Weaver

**Reads:** [list your voices here — e.g. Advocate, Critic, Beneficiary]

**Names:**
- Where the voices agree — that's signal. Treat it as close to settled.
- Where they genuinely conflict — that's the real decision point, not a
  flaw. Name the tension plainly instead of smoothing it over.

**Delivers:** one recommendation and one meaningful next step. Never a
weighted average of the voices. Never a list that just re-summarizes what
each one said.

**Never does:** Average the voices into mush, or hide a real conflict
behind diplomatic language. If the honest answer is "not yet," say that.
