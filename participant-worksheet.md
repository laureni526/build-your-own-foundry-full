# Build Your Own Foundry — Participant Worksheet

*You already have a philosophy. It's trapped in stories you haven't
written down yet. Today you start the system that gets it out.*

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The One Rule (keep this)

> **Nothing becomes a principle until multiple stories from different
> contexts support it.**
>
> When you're tempted to declare something true, the default answer is:
> *Not yet. Keep observing.*

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Part 1 — Story Mining (write 2–3)

Don't find a *good* story. Find a **specific** one. Specific beats
impressive.

Prompts if you're stuck:

-   A moment that changed how you work — that you still think about
-   A time you were sure you were right, and turned out wrong
-   Something you did that worked, that you've never explained to anyone
-   The advice you give most often — and the moment that taught it to you

# Part 3 — My Pattern Hunch (exactly one)

Read your story cards. What keeps repeating? Use this exact format —
it keeps you honest about the evidence:

> **I have a hunch that** ________________________________________
>
> **— but I've only seen it** _______ **times, in** _______
> **context(s).**

A hunch becomes a pattern when new stories, from *different* contexts,
keep confirming it. Until then, it stays a hunch. That's not
self-doubt — that's what makes your certainty checkable.

# Part 6 — My 30-Day Capture Habit

The system only compounds if it's fed. The minimum viable habit:

**One story a week.** 10 minutes. Use the story card structure.

-   My writing day and time: _________________________ *(be exact —
    "Sunday 8pm with coffee" survives; "weekly" doesn't)*
-   Where my stories will live: _________________________ *(notebook,
    doc, notes app — whatever you'll actually open. The system is the
    discipline, not the software.)*

**One pattern check at day 30.** 30 minutes. Reread everything and ask:
*what's repeating?* Promote your hunch to a pattern **only** if new
stories from different contexts backed it.

-   My day-30 review date: _________________________
-   My accountability partner from this room: _________________________

# What You Just Built

| You wrote | In a year it becomes |

| 3 story cards | A story library — 50+ pieces of documented evidence |
| 1 pattern hunch | Named patterns you can teach from |
| 1 question | A sharpened north star (it will change — that's it working) |
| 1 open questions page | The discipline that keeps it all honest |
| 1 weekly habit | The engine |

Somewhere around month three or four, you'll reread your stories and a
principle will be sitting there waiting for you.

**You won't write it. You'll find it.**
